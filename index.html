— 04 / common questions

Things people ask us.

Is Sense-Optimization compatible with Windows 11? +

Yes. We support Windows 10 (21H2+) and Windows 11 on x64 hardware. All tuning profiles are tested against both platforms and updated when Microsoft ships major servicing updates.

Are the changes reversible? +

Every profile is applied through a versioned, reversible system. A single click restores your machine to its prior configuration, and we keep a full audit log of every change made.

Will the overlay work with streaming software? +

The overlay renders through a standard capture-friendly layer and is compatible with OBS, Streamlabs, and similar broadcast tools. You can toggle visibility per-scene.

How does the refund policy work? +

Within 14 days of purchase, contact support for a full refund, no questions asked. For lifetime plans, the same window applies from the date of purchase.

Do you offer volume licensing for studios? +

Yes. For teams of 10+ workstations we offer discounted licenses and centralized deployment. Contact sales@sense-optimization.com for a quote.
